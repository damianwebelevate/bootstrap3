JustU Control Your Digital Self

JustU is the one stop shop for all your social sites and possible more JustU decide.

You will have the ability to receive feeds from your favourite social sites. JustU will have complete customised control over what you want to see and when from your favourite social sites.

The personal private profile page is a digital you where you can build specific content within grouped areas of interest collating photo s, article links with your pinned opinion and your status on your personal digital self. Once built you can then push your content to your social network environments all from within JustU UI.

No need to login to multiple individual social sites to create posts simply build your post(s)/content and once you are happy you choose when to make live.

The benefit to JustU is that it has a one to many relationship (social sites or other) allowing you simplified control, awareness in how you interact within the great digital environment.
